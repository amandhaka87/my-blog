About Me: Aman Dhaka

Hi, I'm Aman Dhaka. I’m a technical learner currently in my 4th semester of BCA, with a deep-rooted passion for automation, cloud infrastructure, and the evolving world of MLOps.

My journey began in the world of DevOps, where I fell in love with the efficiency of Docker, the scale of AWS, and the orchestration power of Kubernetes. But as I built projects like my Python-based voice assistant, I realized that the future of infrastructure isn't just about hosting apps—it's about powering Intelligence.
